Donating Money to the Tunnelblick Project

Thank you for considering making a donation of money!

Although we welcome donations of money, we would prefer that you help us in some other way if you can.

You can donate money with PayPal or a debit or credit card through PayPal. We use PayPal because it is the least horrible payment provider that most Tunnelblick users are comfortable using.
