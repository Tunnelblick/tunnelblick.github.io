Changing Multiple Settings at Once

You can easily change a setting for more than one VPN all at a time.

When you change most Tunnelblick settings, the change is applied to all VPN configurations that are currently selected in the list on the left side of the "Configurations" panel of Tunnelblick's "VPN Details" window. This works with settings that are set on the "Settings" tab, on Tunnelblick's "Advanced Settings" window, and on some settings set on the "gear" menu under the list of configurations.

To select more than one VPN configuration at a time, use standard macOS conventions:

  • To select all configurations, click to select one configuration, hold down the "Command" key (a/k/a the "Apple" or "Cloverleaf" key) while pressing and releasing the "a" key.
  • To add a configuration to the currently selected configurations, hold down the "Command" key while clicking on the name of the configuration.
  • To add all configurations between the currently selected configuration and a new one, hold down one of the "Shift" keys while clicking on the name of the new configuration.
